About Mes

Mechanical Engineering Society of BIT Sindri was established to meet the diversified challenges and bring out innovative approach in the fields of Mechanical engineering. It provides industrial exposure to draw the practical application of concepts and avails its members a platform to enrich their management skills. Under the guidance of HOD Dr. S.K. Singh sir and other associate professors, Mechanical Engineering Society is progressing towards achieving its goals. Animi!

With the motto of holistic development, Mechanical Engineering Society focuses on the personality devlopment and enhancement of technical skills of students in B.I.T Sindri.

Innovating today for a mechanized tomorrow. Join us as we bridge theoretical knowledge with practical excellence, fostering a community of future-ready mechanical engineers who don't just understand machines - they revolutionize them.
