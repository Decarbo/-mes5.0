"YANTRIKA" is the annual techfest that brings many opportunities for budding minds on the campus and outside. It is a platform for students to showcase their technical and managerial skills and explore innovative ideas. The techfest features various events, workshops, exhibitions, and competitions in the fields of engineering, science, technology and management and also provides the opportunity for students to gain exposure to the latest technologies.
