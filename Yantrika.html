Yantrika

"YANTRIKA" is the annual techfest that brings many opportunities for budding minds on the campus and outside. It is a platform for students to showcase their technical and managerial skills and explore innovative ideas. The techfest features various events, workshops, exhibitions, and competitions in the fields of engineering, science, technology and management and also provides the opportunity for students to gain exposure to the latest technologies.

Our Events

Green Olympiad

An environmental awareness competition that tests participants' knowledge about environmental issues, sustainable development, and eco-friendly practices. Join us to showcase your environmental consciousness and win exciting prizes.

Mad CAD

A Computer-Aided Design competition where participants demonstrate their creativity and technical skills in 3D modeling and design. Showcase your engineering vision through innovative designs.

Mech-a-Hunt

An exciting treasure hunt with a mechanical twist! Navigate through challenging puzzles and tasks that test your engineering knowledge and problem-solving skills.

Water Rocket Challenge

Design and launch your own water rocket! Compete with other teams to achieve the highest altitude and most innovative design using basic materials.

Smart Campus

Transform your campus ideas into reality! Propose innovative solutions for making educational institutions smarter and more sustainable.

Accolade

Celebrate excellence in engineering! A platform to recognize and reward outstanding projects, innovations, and achievements in the field of engineering.

Guest Talks

Learn from industry experts and renowned professionals through our series of enlightening guest lectures. Gain insights into the latest trends and developments in engineering.
