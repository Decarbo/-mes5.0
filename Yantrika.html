Yantrika

"YANTRIKA" is the annual techfest that brings many opportunities for budding minds on the campus and outside. It is a platform for students to showcase their technical and managerial skills and explore innovative ideas. The techfest features various events, workshops, exhibitions, and competitions in the fields of engineering, science, technology and management and also provides the opportunity for students to gain exposure to the latest technologies.

Our Events

EXHIBIZONE

Unleash your creative prowess at EXHIBIZONE – a celebration of engineering marvels and mechanical mastery! Witness live demonstrations, hands-on models, and futuristic concepts brought to life by brilliant minds. This is your chance to showcase, explore, and inspire innovation.

MECHIE OF THE YEAR

A battle of brilliance where the finest mechanical minds compete for the ultimate title – Mechie of the Year! Display your technical knowledge, problem-solving skills, and all-round excellence in this dynamic showdown of intellect and innovation.

MECH-A-HUNT

Gear up for an adrenaline-pumping mechanical treasure hunt! Solve puzzles, crack codes, and race against time in MECH-A-HUNT – a thrilling journey through the world of machines and mystery.

WATER ROCKET CHALLENGE

Design. Launch. Compete. The WATER ROCKET CHALLENGE lets you engineer your own rocket and aim for the skies! Combine physics with creativity to outperform in this fun, splash-filled contest of trajectory and precision.

GUEST TALKS

The Mechanical Engineering Society at BIT Sindri, Dhanbad, hosted inspiring guest talks under the banner of Yantrika, aimed at enriching the academic experience of students. Through Yantrika Guest Talks, students gained valuable exposure to real-world applications, emerging trends, and challenges in the mechanical engineering domain.

GREEN OLYMPIAD

The Green Olympiad is a prestigious event organized by the Mechanical Engineering Society at BIT Sindri, Dhanbad. It is a platform for students to showcase their innovative solutions to environmental challenges. The competition encourages creativity, teamwork, and practical application of engineering principles to address real-world problems.

SMART CAMPUS

The Smart Campus initiative is a comprehensive project designed to transform the BIT Sindri campus into a state-of-the-art, sustainable, and technologically advanced environment. This initiative aims to integrate cutting-edge technologies, smart infrastructure, and innovative solutions to enhance the learning experience and operational efficiency of the institution.

MAD CAD

The MAD CAD event is a celebration of creativity and innovation in the field of mechanical design. It showcases the best projects and designs created by students using CAD software, highlighting their technical skills and design capabilities.
